Workshop - Tools, Password Profiling, Brute Forcing

Abstract

Kim will take us through the collection of tools added and configured on his penetration testing machine used throughout his book (Holistic Info-Sec for Web Developers). Kim will then profile a well known celebrities password, creating a short-list, then (on-line) brute force their login. Come along, it’ll be fun.
